Non Urgent / Non Airbnb Cases
Complaint Workflow
When to use this: Use this process when a case is lower risk — we can afford to wait for a partner response and we do not want to lose Jetstream funds unnecessarily. There is also a lower risk of being penalised by the OTA.

With this flow, we ask the partner to compensate the guest first and wait for their approval, following our normal workflow. We only escalate to TL after approximately 48 hours of no response, or if the partner refuses — at which point TL reviews whether Jetstream should risk covering the cost or not.
